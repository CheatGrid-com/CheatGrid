# Streaks 🔥

A daily streak is the simplest way to build a study habit, and it works across the whole app.

## How it works

You earn a **streak point on any day you study**, in any of three ways: clear a flashcard session, complete a practice test, or spend **20+ active minutes reading cheat sheets** that day (that reading time is counted per day, so it doesn't carry over from yesterday). Only the first counts each day, so you can't farm it, and any kind of studying keeps it going. Do a little every day and your streak climbs.

## Lives ♥♥♥

Real life happens, so you don't lose everything the moment you miss a day. You start each streak with **3 lives**. Miss a day and you spend a life instead of breaking the streak. Only when you run out of lives *and* miss another day does the streak reset (your best is always remembered).

**Hit a milestone, earn a bonus life.** Reach streak milestones (3, 7, 14, 30, 60, 100 days and well beyond) and you're awarded an extra life (up to a maximum of 10). The longer you keep going, the more cushion you build up, so a long streak gets harder to lose.

## The Streak page

Open **Streak 🔥** from your account menu for the full picture:

- a flame showing your **current streak**, your **lives**, and progress toward your **next milestone**
- a **milestones** row that lights up as you reach each one
- quick stats (longest streak, total active days, this month) and a peek at your habits
- a **calendar and heatmap** so you can see every day you studied at a glance

## Tip

Pair it with a short daily **[flashcard](intelligent-flashcards.md)** Review. It's usually just a few minutes, and it's the easiest way to never miss a day.
